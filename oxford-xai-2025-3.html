Overview

The Oxford: Advances and Applications of Explainable Artificial Intelligence forum brings together leading researchers, clinicians, and industry innovators to discuss the latest developments and real‑world applications of explainable AI. This half‑day international event features a keynote, invited talks, industry presentations, and opportunities for collaboration.

In‑person · Free registration · Limited seats Academic + Industry Networking & Coffee
